Now

Another chapter of my life comes to an end, as I leave the place I've called home for the past 2 years today. I don't feel sad - I think I'm ready to move forward. But the funny thing about change is, even though we need it, and we can't stop it, it leaves you with a little seed of melancholy that always sprouts on long car drives or when you're alone and looking at the sea.
