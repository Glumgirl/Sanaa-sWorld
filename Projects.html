Hello Neighbours!

I co-designed and led the project, “Hello neighbours!” which created a living resource for residents, architects and stakeholders. It uncovers the relationship between spaces and neighbourly relations, giving a formal framework to these seemingly intangible relationships.This is the first step towards developing community resilience and the capacity to respond to crises on a hyper-local level..

Sara & The Mysterious Hacker Club

Pretia Inc. is a Tokyo-based early stage Augmented Reality (AR) startup. Mostly known for their placebased AR puzzle games, they are also developing an AR development program called AR Cloud.In 2019, I localised the first and most popular game, “Sara and the Mysterious Hacker Club”. This involved re-designing the puzzles for a western audience, scouting locations in Shibuya to be used, and conducting play tests with the target market. As a side project, I also worked on creating promotional AR content for the game..

Around the Corner

Alongside my project partner, I co-led Around the Corner, a research and design project that gets young people engaged in the design of their local built environment, through a series of hands-on workshops. These workshops utilised bespoke methods that forge a link to their tacit and embodied knowledge of public spaces, specifically in Southwark, and a finalised design for new public seating in Peckham.
